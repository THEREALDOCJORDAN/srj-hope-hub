S.M.A.R.T. Recovery

S.M.A.R.T. (Self-Management and Recovery Training) Recovery is a science-based program that helps individuals overcome addictive behaviors. This group focuses on self-empowerment and self-reliance, using cognitive-behavioral techniques and non-confrontational motivational methods.

Key aspects of S.M.A.R.T. Recovery include:

  • Building and maintaining motivation
  • Coping with urges
  • Managing thoughts, feelings, and behaviors
  • Living a balanced life
